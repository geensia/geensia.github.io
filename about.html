My Story

Hi, I’m Geensia — a Hmong digital designer and communicator who wants to help bring ideas to life.

Growing up, I didn’t have a traditional childhood. Instead, I grew up in my parents’ small Asian market in Valdese, North Carolina. Because I was often there, I grew an interest in helping people understand things. Whether it was explaining how technology works to my parents, designing a simple menu for customers, or finding ways to explain complex information, I enjoyed being the bridge between information and people. That passion grew with me over the years and I realized I could make a career out of it.

I recently graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a B.S. in Information Science, a B.A. in Communication (New Media), and a minor in Studio Arts. Across these areas, I’ve learned how to combine technology, communication, and design to turn ideas into experiences that are both creative and easy to understand.

Through my work, I hope to help others better understand the ideas around them.
