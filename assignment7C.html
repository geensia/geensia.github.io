ABOUT US

Xieng Khouang Market Place is named after one of the Laos provinces, where both of the owners grew up. Unfortantely, when they were preteens, they had to migrate to refugee camps and eventually to the US due to the Secret War. It has a special place in their hearts since they had to leave their loved ones. To honor their ancestors and culture, they decided to open a little grocery store in Valdese, NC. This became their new home.

Mom's Eggroll

Xieng Khouang Market Place is home to Mom's Eggroll since summer of 2019. What makes this food different, especially the eggrolls, is Mom's secret recipe.
