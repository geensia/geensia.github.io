Highlands

What started out as a only farm has now grown to become a resturant and butcher shop: Highlands Butchery. It is located a bit outside of Morganton, however, it is the only custom shop in Eastern Burke County, so it's definitely worth going! All their dishes are grown from the farm they have. It ranges from burgers, to burritos, salads, and even delicious breakfast food.
