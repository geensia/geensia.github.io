Welcome to

Lake Rhodhiss

Lake Rhodhiss is a pristine lake located at the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Western North Carolina. The lake was created by Duke Power Company and is popular for its water clarity and accessibility to areas like Hickory, Charlotte, Asheville, Winston-Salem and the lovely mountains in Boone. Lake Rhodhiss NC is a popular place to enjoy swimming, boating, water skiing, and fishing. In fact, there are many fishing tournaments held on the lake, as it is home to a variety of different species of fish.

Fishing!

We hope your fishing trip is good. Good luck and we hope you have a full catch! Largemouth Bass Tips: If you have a chance to get out on the water, you are going to have to fish deep and slow. Jig fishing and Plastic baits may be your best bet this time of year for largemouth bass. You may also try fishing some sinking jerk baits.
