Project Overview

I worked as a Residential Computing Consultant (RCC) ResNET during my time at UNC-Chapel Hill, where I helped students living on-campus connect their devices to Wi-Fi, ethernet activations, and any other technical assistance they may need. In addition to my role, I was also on the marketing team for ResNET.

Each month, I designed two to three social media posts covering topics such as RCC spotlights, campus printing resources, and technology tips for students. I also developed a brand guidelines document to help future marketing team members maintain a consistent visual identity and messaging strategy across ResNET's communications.
