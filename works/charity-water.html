Project Overview

Charity: Water is a nonprofit that helps bring clean and safe water to communities in need. I developed a digital marketing campaign designed to increase awareness and engagement among college students. The project included designing a user-centered landing page, creating organic social media content based on strategic content pillars, developing a TikTok influencer marketing plan, and building a paid social media strategy that incorporated both targeted and retargeting campaigns. Each element was designed to connect with the target audience and encourage meaningful action in support of the organization's mission.

Throughout this project, I strengthened my skills in audience research, campaign strategy, content marketing, social media planning, and user-centered design while learning how to create cohesive marketing initiatives across multiple digital channels.
