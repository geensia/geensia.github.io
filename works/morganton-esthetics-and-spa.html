Project Overview

Morganton Esthetics & Spa was one of my first freelance projects outside of my family’s business. They are a small beauty and wellness business that provides professional esthetics and spa services. For this project, I began by creating business cards and brochures to highlight the spa’s services and contact information. I then redesigned their website to clearly showcase their offerings and create an accessible, welcoming online presence for potential clients.

Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with the client to understand their brand and ensure visual consistency across all materials. This experience strengthened my skills in client communication, branding, and project management.
