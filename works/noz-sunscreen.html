Project Overview

Nöz Sunscreen is a sunscreen brand focused on creativity and environmental responsibility. I designed and developed a Shopify store for their business. Then I created a customer persona to guide branding decisions and designed a landing page that reflected the brand's artistic identity while encouraging customer engagement. The project included developing a value proposition, selecting visual branding elements, conducting SEO keyword research, and optimizing the user experience through strategic call-to-action buttons, trust badges, and customer reviews. I also proposed an A/B test to evaluate how different CTA colors could influence conversion rates.

During this project, I gained hands-on experience applying digital marketing principles in a real-world setting, strengthening my skills in branding, SEO, user experience design, conversion optimization, and e-commerce website development.
