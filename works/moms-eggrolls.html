Project Overview

Mom’s Eggrolls is a local Asian food truck based in Valdese, NC, known for their freshly made eggrolls. To strengthen their customer experience and brand presence, I designed a new menu that was easy to read, visually engaging, and aligned with their brand identity.

Through this project, I gained experience in designing for real-world customer interactions, learning how layout, typography, and visual hierarchy can help customers quickly find information and make ordering decisions.
